Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a very tasty food, famous at italian cuicine, made with beef or beef/pork, presented on alternated layers of pasta, beef or beef/pork and bechamel sauce.

On this page we will show you how to cook the tastiest lasagna you will ever eat!!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Bechamel
  • Pasta

Directions

  1. Put a layer of pasta on the bottom of a pyrex.
  2. Put a layer of meat.
  3. Pur a layer of bechamel.
  4. Repeat the previous steps 3-5 times, you will end with a layer of pasta and bechamel over the pasta layer
  5. Cook for 35 minutes on a preheated oven at 300 degrees
  6. Enjoy!!!
