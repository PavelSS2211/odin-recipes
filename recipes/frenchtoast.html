French toast recipe

Description

French toast is an easy but tasty way to have a breakfast, or even a dessert.

This recipe will give you an easy and fast way to make them!!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Milk
  • Box bread

Directions

  1. Put the eggs on a bowl and whisk them slowly.
  2. Add milk, vanilla and cinnamon while you whisk the eggs.
  3. Grease a skillet with the butter and cook the bread until it's brown on both sides.
  4. Serve warm.
  5. Enjoy!!!
